My all time Favourites

I’d recommend everyone to watch these at least once in their lifetimes.

42 (2013)

added on 07 Feb, 2025

The story of the baseball legend Jackie Robinson’s journey starting out in the MLB, highlighting the racism in the sport. All the leads have done phenomenal acting, and this is a movie you won’t forget anytime soon.

Fun fact - This is the movie that earned Chadwick Boseman his role as Black Panther, and rightfully so. May he rest in peace.

Watch 42

Arcane (2021)

added on 18 Jan, 2025

I think I speak for the majority when I say this is one of the greatest web series you’ll ever experience.

The storytelling, character development, animation, dialogues, score - everything is done masterfully. And with a total runtime of 12 hours, it just feels like a long, well-made, engaging movie.
I honestly feel grateful to witness it in my lifetime, and I am yet to encounter another series that moves me as much as Arcane does.

Watch Arcane

Shutter Island (2010)

added on 18 Jan, 2025

I love how Scorsese directed this. Everything unfolds as it should, keeping the watcher engaged all the time - even during rewatches.

If you wanna get your mind blown, this is the way.

Watch Shutter Island

Dead Poets Society (1989)

added on 08 Jan, 2024

I’ve always had a soft spot for this movie. It goes into the topic of finding one’s true passion, realising what you’re really meant to do in this world, and what you might have to overco…..
It’s a good movie, and you should watch it. You’ll either love it to the core, or consider it another overrated mediocre relic of the past; be your own judge.

Watch Dead Poets Society
