My diet

This diet is written with the aim of reducing pimples and building muscle, along with the constraint of being in college hostel (semester 6).

Meals

  • 4L+ water
  • ~10k steps
  • dry fruits
  • dried cranberries
  • curd and salad in mess lunch
  • muscle menu dinner
  • plain boiled eggs at usha
  • fruits, more dry fruits

Right now, just build the discipline of eating clean regularly, and then increase necessary food items intake to bulk or complete protein.
