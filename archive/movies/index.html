Movies & Shows

my all time favourites



I'd recommend everyone to watch these atleast once in their lifetimes.

Arcane (2021 - 2024)

18 Jan, 2025


I think I speak for the majority when I say this is one of the greatest webseries you'll ever experience.
The storytelling, character development, animation, dialogues, score, everything is done masterfully. And with a total runtime of 12 hrs, it just feels like a long well-made engaging movie.
I honestly feel grateful to be able to witness it in my lifetime, and I am yet to encounter another series that moves me as much as arcane does.
Watch Arcane


Shutter Island (2011)

18 Jan 2025


I love how Scorsese directed this. Everything unfolds as it should, and keeps the watcher engaged all the time. Even during rewatches.
If you wanna get your mind fucked, this is the way.
Watch Shutter Island


Dead Poets Society (1989)

08 Jan 2024


This one-of-a-kind movie is one that celebrates individuality, free thinking, and the importance of seizing the moment, all while critiquing conformity and societal pressures.
A simple yet impactful story that inspires and resonates deeply.
Watch Dead Poets Society
