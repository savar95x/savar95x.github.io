Split

  1. Shoulders, Quads
    Lateral Raise, OHP; Incline Curl, Pinwheel Curl, Leg Press
  2. Back
    Pushdown, DB extension; Pulldown, Bent Over Rows, Reverse Pec Deck
  3. Rest
  4. Chest and Shoulders
    Lateral Raise, Bench Press, Pec Deck; Incline Curl, Pinwheel Curl, OHP
  5. RDL
    Pushdown, DB extension; RDL, Pullover

Notes

  • The ‘;’ separates the morning and the evening session, morning intended to being slightly easier
  • Some form of cardio (incline walk / cycling / eliptical) is done every time I hit the gym
  • Food and Rest are prioritised (130g+ protein, 7hrs+ sleep)
  • Evolving Rep Range is followed on all exercises

That’s all.

It takes time, but efforts pay off, if the efforts are with good intention.
