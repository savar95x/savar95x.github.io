This is my workout routine I am currently running. To check out my older plans, click here.

Priority

  • Back
  • Shoulders
  • Arms
  • Lower Body Fat

Exercise Selection

Back

Pullup or Pulldown (depending on my ability to pull my bodyweight) 6-12
Barbell Bent Over Rows 6-10

Shoulders

Standing Lateral Raise 15-20
Seated Smith Machine Overhead Press 6-10
Reverse Pec Deck 15+

Arms

Skull Crusher 5-10 JM Press 6-10
Dumbbell Preacher Curl 8-12
Hammer Curl 6-10
Kitana Extensions (Left Tricep) 8-12

Chest

Dips or Bench (depending on my ability to dip my bodyweight) 6-10

Quads

High Bar Barbell Squats 5-8

Hamstrings

Romanian Deadlift 5-8

Calves, Abs, Upper Traps

Standing Calf Raises 8-12
Decline Sit Ups 6+
DB shrugs 10+

Body Part Split

  1. Back, Shoulders
  2. Squat, Arms
  3. Rest
  4. Chest, Shoulders
  5. RDL, Arms
  6. Rest

Split

  1. Back and Shoulders
    Lateral Raise, OHP; Pulldown, Bent Over Rows, Rev Pec Deck,
  2. Squat Day
    Skull Crusher SS Calves, Preacher Curl SS Calves; Squat, Abs SS Shrugs
  3. Rest
  4. Chest and Shoulders
    Bench Press; Lateral Raise, OHP, Rev Pec Deck
  5. RDL and Arms
    Cable Overhead Extension SS Calves, Hammer Curl SS Calves; RDL, Abs SS Shrugs
  6. Rest

Notes

  • The ‘;’ separates the morning and the evening session, morning intended to being slightly easier
  • Some form of cardio (incline running / cycling / skipping) is done every time I hit the gym
  • Food and Rest are prioritised
  • Evolving Rep Range is followed on all exercises

That’s all.

It takes time, but efforts pay off, if the efforts are with good intention.
