Workout

my current workout split



I follow a push, pull, leg inspired split.

Push (chest, bicep, front and side delt)

  • incline db press ss barbell curl
  • standing bb overhead press
  • hammer curl ss lateral raises
  • decline situps

Pull (back, tricep, hamstrings)

  • cable high row ss skull crushers
  • neutral grip pulldown ss tricep
  • db rows ss jm press
  • leg curls

Legs (quads, calves, rear delt, upper traps)

  • squats
  • standing calf raises
  • rear delt row ss db shrugs
  • obliques

SS means superset. No direct work for neck or forearms right now.
Every movement is failed in the 5-10 rep range. And performed for 2-4 sets, depending on how I feel that day.
Rest days are flexible depending on how I feel and clashes with other responsibilities.
