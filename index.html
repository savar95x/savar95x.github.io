About

I am a third year undergraduate student pursuing Electrical Engineering at IIT Madras. I have a deep passion for natural hypertrophy training, am an avid free software and linux enthusiast, and a cinema lover.

During my leisure hours, I often find myself working on this website or on my rice. Apart from that, I spend time trying to understand various training methodologies and philosophies in natural bodybuilding.

I started this blog as a platform to express my thoughts, articulate my ideas, and hopefully find solace through the process of writing.
